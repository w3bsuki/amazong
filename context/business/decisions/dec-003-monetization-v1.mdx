# DEC-003: Monetization v1 (Buyer Protection Hybrid)

## Context

Seller commissions in C2C marketplaces are often bypassed via off-platform deals.

## Decision

- Personal sellers pay **0% seller fee**.
- Buyers pay a **Buyer Protection** fee (transparent).
- Businesses pay a **small seller fee** and get lower buyer fees.
- Fees are **plan-driven** via `subscription_plans`.

## Why

- Matches proven EU pattern (Vinted)
- Keeps seller friction low
- Ties monetization to a clear value proposition (trust + support)
