# Data Room Checklist (Investor-Ready)

This is a **checklist**, not a request to publish sensitive data publicly.

## Corporate

- company registration and shareholder structure
- cap table (current + pro-forma after round)
- board/manager appointments and resolutions

## Financial

- 12–24 month forecast assumptions (inputs, not just outputs)
- monthly P&L and cashflow (once live)
- unit economics model (order-level)

## Product & tech

- product roadmap (V1 → V2)
- architecture overview and key dependencies
- security posture summary (admin access, RLS, secrets handling)

## Payments & compliance

- Stripe integration overview (Connect, webhooks, refunds)
- chargeback handling playbook
- KYC/KYB approach (Stripe + platform controls)

## Legal/policies (drafts + counsel review status)

- terms of service (draft)
- privacy policy (draft)
- returns/refunds policy
- prohibited items policy
- moderation/enforcement policy

## Operations

- support workflows and SLAs
- dispute resolution workflow + evidence handling
- risk register with owners

## Metrics & traction

- KPI dashboard definitions and actuals (when live)
- cohort retention (buyers/sellers)
- trust metrics (refunds, disputes, chargebacks)
