# Competitor Analysis

Market landscape for Bulgarian classifieds.

## Direct Competitors

### OLX.bg

**Market leader** in Bulgarian classifieds.

| Aspect | OLX | Amazong |
|--------|-----|---------|
| Market share | ~70% | New entrant |
| Listings | 500K+ | Building |
| Mobile app | Yes | Planned |
| Monetization | Ads + Premium | Subscriptions |

**Weaknesses to exploit:**
- Outdated UI/UX
- Slow mobile experience
- Aggressive advertising
- Limited seller tools

### Bazar.bg

Secondary player, focused on B2B.

**Weaknesses:**
- Poor mobile experience
- Limited features
- Dated design

### Facebook Marketplace

Growing presence in BG market.

**Weaknesses:**
- No dedicated support
- Limited seller tools
- Privacy concerns

## Competitive Advantages

### 1. Modern UX

- Clean, fast interface
- Mobile-first design
- Intuitive navigation

### 2. Seller Tools

- Analytics dashboard
- Bulk listing management
- Automated repricing

### 3. Trust Features

- Verified sellers
- Buyer protection
- Escrow payments

### 4. Fair Pricing

- Transparent fees
- No hidden costs
- Value-based tiers

## Positioning

**"The modern marketplace for Bulgaria"**

- Target: Tech-savvy users frustrated with OLX
- Value prop: Better UX, fair pricing, seller tools
- Differentiator: Trust & transparency
