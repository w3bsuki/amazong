# Business Overview

Business and product strategy for Treido Marketplace.

## Launch posture (V1)

- **On-platform checkout** (Stripe) + **Stripe Connect payouts**
- **Buyer Protection fee** (transparent) + **business seller fee** (small) as the core model
- **No COD**
- Keep fees low to avoid off-platform leakage
