# Terms of Service (Draft)

This is a working draft for Treido Marketplace. It is **not legal advice**.

## 1) Scope

Treido provides a marketplace platform including:

- listings and discovery,
- buyer ↔ seller messaging,
- on-platform checkout,
- order status lifecycle,
- support and dispute handling.

These terms apply to **personal** and **business** accounts.

## 2) Accounts

- You must provide accurate account information and keep your account secure.
- You are responsible for activity on your account.
- We may restrict or suspend accounts to protect users, comply with law, or enforce policies.

## 3) Listings and selling rules

- Listings must be accurate (photos, description, condition, authenticity).
- You must have the right to sell the item and it must be lawful.
- Prohibited items are not allowed.

See: [Prohibited Items](/policies/prohibited-items).

## 4) Fees and plans

Treido’s fee model includes:

- a **Buyer Protection** fee paid by the buyer,
- a **business seller fee** (business accounts only),
- optional subscriptions and boosts.

See:

- [Monetization](/business/monetization)
- [Plans & Pricing](/business/pricing)

## 5) Payments and payout release

- Payments are processed via Stripe.
- For launch, **cash on delivery (COD)** is not supported.
- Seller payouts are handled via Stripe Connect and may be released after delivery confirmation and/or dispute resolution (escrow-style logic).

See: [Payments](/payments).

## 6) Shipping, delivery, and cancellations

- Sellers must ship within defined timelines.
- Buyers must confirm receipt or report issues within defined windows.
- Cancellations and refunds follow the returns/refunds policy.

See: [Returns & Refunds](/policies/returns).

## 7) Disputes and support

- Buyers can open disputes for eligible issues (e.g. item not received, damaged, not as described).
- Both parties must provide evidence within the defined timelines.
- Treido may decide outcomes in line with policy to protect platform safety and payment integrity.

See: [Refunds & disputes (payments)](/payments/refunds-disputes) and [Support Playbook](/business/ops/support-playbook).

## 8) Ratings and reviews

- Users may be able to leave ratings/reviews after completed orders.
- We may remove content that violates policies or is abusive.

## 9) Enforcement

We may take enforcement actions such as removing listings, limiting features, or banning accounts for:

- prohibited items,
- fraud or chargeback abuse,
- harassment,
- repeated policy violations.

## 10) Liability and limitations

Treido is a platform service. Users remain responsible for their listings, communications, and compliance with applicable law.

## 11) Changes

We may update these terms and will provide appropriate notice when required.
