# Terms of Service (Draft)

Internal outline for marketplace terms. Not legal advice.

## Scope

- Marketplace providing listings, messaging, checkout, escrow-like release, and support.
- Applies to personal and business accounts.

## Accounts

- Eligibility (age, jurisdiction)
- Account security and responsibilities
- Enforcement actions (suspension, bans)

## Listings

- Accuracy requirements (photos, descriptions, condition)
- Prohibited items (see policy)
- Intellectual property / counterfeit policy

## Fees

- Buyer Protection fee (buyer-paid)
- Business seller fee (seller-paid)
- Subscription plans and billing terms

## Payments and escrow-style release

- Payment via Stripe only (no COD in V1)
- Conditions for payout release and refunds

## Disputes

- How to open a dispute
- Evidence requirements and timelines
- Resolution outcomes

## Liability & limitations

- Platform limitations; user responsibility for listings
- Chargeback/abuse handling

## Changes

- How we update terms and notify users
