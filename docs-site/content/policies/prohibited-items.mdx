# Prohibited Items Policy

> Canonical Prohibited Items policy for Treido.

---

## Overview

Treido does not allow listings that violate law or create unacceptable safety or fraud risk.

## Common prohibited categories

Examples of prohibited categories include:

- illegal items, stolen goods
- weapons and weapon parts where restricted by law
- counterfeit goods and IP-infringing items
- hazardous materials and unsafe products
- adult/explicit content where restricted

## Enforcement

Treido may remove listings, restrict accounts, or report activity when required by law or safety policy.
