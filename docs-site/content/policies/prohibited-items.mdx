# Prohibited Items

This list is a baseline; expand as needed for Bulgaria/EU compliance.

## Always prohibited

- Illegal goods (drugs, weapons, etc.)
- Counterfeit goods and replicas
- Stolen items
- Hacked accounts, digital credentials
- Adult content involving minors (report to authorities immediately)

## Restricted / requires review

- Alcohol / tobacco products
- Medical devices and regulated products
- High-risk digital goods (gift cards, codes)

## Enforcement

- Listing removal
- Account suspension/ban
- Funds hold where allowed by policy/provider
