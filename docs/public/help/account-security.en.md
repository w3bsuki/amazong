# Account Security (Help)

> How to keep your Treido account secure.

---

## basics: Basics

- Use a strong, unique password.
- Do not share your login details.
- Keep your email account secure (email access can be used to reset passwords).

## suspicious-activity: Suspicious activity

If you suspect unauthorized access:

1) Change your password immediately.
2) Review recent activity (orders, messages, listings).
3) Contact support as soon as possible.
