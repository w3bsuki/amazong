# Bulgarian Online Marketplace Market Research

**Prepared for:** Treido.eu  
**Date:** January 17, 2026  
**Focus:** Competitive analysis for Bulgarian C2C/B2C marketplace entry

---

## Executive Summary

The Bulgarian online marketplace sector is dominated by OLX.bg (part of OLX Group/Prosus) and Bazar.bg (owned by Резон ООД). As of January 2026, Bulgaria has transitioned to the Euro, creating a unique moment for market disruption. The e-commerce market is projected at ~€1.7-2.7 billion with 53-65% user penetration, presenting significant opportunity for a well-positioned new entrant.

### 4.1 OLX.bg

**Strengths:**
- Market leader in classifieds (~4M monthly users)

- Simpler, more transparent pricing

## 5. Bulgarian E-commerce Regulations

### 5.1 Закон за електронната търговия (Law on Electronic Commerce)

Key requirements:
- Clear identification of the seller (name, address, VAT)
- Transparent pricing (including all taxes and delivery costs)
- Right of withdrawal (14 days for consumers)
- Clear terms and conditions
- Order confirmation requirements
- Data protection compliance

### 5.2 GDPR in Bulgaria

**Supervisory Authority:** КЗЛД (Комисия за защита на личните данни)
- Website: [cpdp.bg](https://cpdp.bg)
- Address: бул. „Проф. Цветан Лазаров" № 2, София 1592

**Key Requirements:**
- Privacy policy in Bulgarian
- Cookie consent (КЗЛД enforces GDPR cookie requirements)
- Data breach notification within 72 hours
- DPO (ДЛЗД) appointment if processing large-scale personal data
- Lawful basis for processing
- Right to be forgotten implementation

### 5.3 Consumer Protection (Закон за защита на потребителите)

- 14-day withdrawal right for online purchases
- 2-year legal guarantee on goods
- Clear information on seller identity
- Pre-contractual information requirements
- Ban on unfair commercial practices

### 5.4 E-commerce Challenges (Trade.gov Report)

Common issues identified:
- Technical malfunctions during ordering/payment
- Delivery speed slower than indicated
- Difficulty finding warranty/legal rights information
- Wrong or damaged goods delivered

## 6. Market Entry Strategy Insights

### 6.1 Euro Transition Opportunity (January 2026)

**Timing Advantage:** Bulgaria adopted the Euro on January 1, 2026. This creates:
- Payment system transitions (competitors adjusting)
- Price redenomination confusion
- Marketing opportunity ("Euro-ready from day one")
- Potential to capture users frustrated with transition issues

### 6.2 Differentiation Opportunities

| Area | Current Gap | Treido Opportunity |
|------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Pricing Transparency** | Complex tiers | Simple, clear pricing |
