## 8. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right of Access (Art. 15)

You have the right to receive:
- Confirmation whether we process your data
- A copy of your personal data
- Information about processing

**How:** Account settings → "Download my data" or email to privacy@treido.eu

### 8.2 Right to Rectification (Art. 16)

You can correct inaccurate data:
- Directly from profile settings
- By request to support

### 8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") (Art. 17)

You can request erasure when:
- Data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw consent
- Data was processed unlawfully

**Exceptions:** We cannot delete data if:
- Law requires retention
- Needed for legal disputes

### 8.4 Right to Restriction (Art. 18)

You can restrict processing if:
- You contest accuracy (pending verification)
- Processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion
- You need data for legal claims

### 8.5 Right to Data Portability (Art. 20)

You have the right to receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) and transfer it to another provider.

### 8.6 Right to Object (Art. 21)

You can object to:
- **Direct marketing** — always and without explanation
- **Processing for legitimate interests** — we will assess the grounds

### 8.7 Rights Regarding Automated Decision-Making (Art. 22)

If we use automated decisions with significant impact, you have the right to:
- Human intervention
- Explanation of the logic
- Contest the decision

**Currently:** We do not use fully automated decisions with legal effects.

### 8.8 How to Exercise Your Rights

1. **Self-service:** Many actions are available from account settings
2. **Email:** privacy@treido.eu
3. **Form:** [treido.eu/privacy-request] (when available)

**Response:** Within **30 days** (may be extended to 60 for complex requests)

**Identification:** We may request identity verification
