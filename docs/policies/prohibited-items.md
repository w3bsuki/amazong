# Prohibited Items and Services — Treido.eu

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

---

## Overview

Treido.eu is committed to being a safe, legal, and trustworthy marketplace. This document lists items and services that are **prohibited** or **restricted** on our platform.

Violations may result in:
- Listing removal
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban
- Report to authorities (for illegal items)

---

## 1. Absolutely Prohibited (Never Allowed)

### 1.1 Illegal Drugs and Substances

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Narcotics and controlled substances (cannabis, cocaine, heroin, etc.)
- Drug paraphernalia (pipes, bongs, syringes marketed for drug use)
- Precursor chemicals (for drug manufacturing)
- Synthetic drugs and "legal highs"
- Prescription medications without license
- Performance-enhancing drugs (steroids) without prescription

### 1.2 Weapons and Ammunition

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Firearms (guns, pistols, rifles) — licensed dealers excluded
- Ammunition and magazines
- Explosives and explosive devices
- Silencers and suppressors
- Tasers and stun guns
- Brass knuckles and switchblades
- Pepper spray over legal limit

⚠️ **May be allowed with restrictions:**
- Deactivated/decorative weapons (with certification)
- Antique weapons (manufactured before 1900)
- Airsoft/BB guns (age-restricted, specific category)
- Kitchen knives and tools (non-weapons)

### 1.3 Stolen and Fraudulent Items

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Stolen goods
- Items with removed serial numbers
- Goods obtained through fraud
- Items under court seizure
- Property subject to disputes/lawsuits

### 1.4 Counterfeit and Replica Goods

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Counterfeit designer items (fake Louis Vuitton, Rolex, etc.)
- Replica luxury goods
- Bootleg media (pirated software, movies, music)
- Fake documents (IDs, diplomas, certificates)
- Items infringing trademarks or copyrights

### 1.5 Dangerous and Hazardous Items

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Toxic chemicals and poisons
- Radioactive materials
- Asbestos-containing products
- Recalled products (safety recalls)
- Expired medical/food products
- Unregulated dietary supplements

---

## 2. Restricted Items (Conditions Apply)

### 2.1 Alcohol

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Private individuals: **PROHIBITED** to sell
- Licensed businesses: Allowed with:
  - Valid alcohol license displayed
  - Age verification process
  - Compliance with local regulations

### 2.2 Tobacco and Vaping Products

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Cigarettes and tobacco: **PROHIBITED** (individual sales)
- E-cigarettes and vapes: **PROHIBITED** to individuals
- Licensed retailers only (with documentation)
- No sales to minors under any circumstances

### 2.3 Vehicles

⚠️ **Allowed with requirements:**
- Must have valid registration/documentation
- Must disclose known defects
- Must not have outstanding liens
- Boats, motorcycles: Same requirements
- Parts: Must be legally obtained

### 2.4 Real Estate

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Sellers must prove ownership or authorization
- No illegal constructions
- Must disclose encumbrances
- Business verification may be required

### 2.5 Electronics with Restrictions

⚠️ **Restrictions apply to:**
- Mobile phones: Must not be blacklisted (IMEI check)
- Game consoles: Must not be modified for piracy
- Satellite equipment: Must comply with regulations
- Radio equipment: Must be certified for EU

### 2.6 Medical Devices

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Home medical devices: Allowed (blood pressure monitors, etc.)
- Prescription devices: **PROHIBITED**
- Used medical equipment: Hygiene disclosure required
- Medical consumables: Must be sealed/unexpired

---

## 3. Adult Content

### 3.1 Sexual Content

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Pornographic material
- Sex toys and adult products
- Sexual services
- Content depicting minors (absolutely prohibited)
- Escort or dating services

### 3.2 Violence and Hate

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Items glorifying violence
- Nazi memorabilia and hate symbols
- Racist, sexist, or discriminatory content
- Torture devices
- Items promoting terrorism

## 4. Living Beings

### 4.1 Animals

❌ **Generally Prohibited:**
- Live animals (most species)
- Endangered species products (ivory, fur from CITES species)
- Fighting animals
- Animals as prizes/gifts

⚠️ **May be allowed:**
- Pet supplies and accessories
- Aquarium fish (specific regulations)
- Licensed breeders (with documentation)

### 4.2 Plants

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Seeds of controlled plants: **PROHIBITED**
- Invasive species: **PROHIBITED**
- Regular plants: Generally allowed
- Protected species: Must comply with regulations

### 4.3 Human-Related

❌ **Completely Prohibited:**
- Human organs, tissues, body parts
- Blood and bodily fluids
- Human remains
- Items made from human materials

## 6. Financial and Investment Items

### 6.1 Currency and Financial Instruments

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Counterfeit currency
- Pyramid/Ponzi schemes
- Unregistered securities
- Cryptocurrency mining contracts (without disclosure)
- Credit card skimmers/fraud tools

⚠️ **Restricted:**
- Cryptocurrency hardware wallets: Allowed if new/sealed
- Collectible coins: Allowed
- Foreign currency: Allowed at reasonable quantities

### 6.2 Gift Cards and Vouchers

⚠️ **Restrictions:**
- Must be legitimate (not stolen/fraudulent)
- Face value must be clearly stated
- Expired cards not allowed
- No significant markup over face value

## 7. Food and Consumables

### 7.1 Food Items

⚠️ **General Rules:**
- Must not be expired
- Must be properly packaged
- Home-made food: Generally **PROHIBITED** (health regulations)
- Dietary supplements: Must be EU-approved

❌ **Prohibited:**
- Expired food products
- Unsealed/tampered products
- Products without proper labeling
- Prohibited additives

### 7.2 Baby Products

⚠️ **Extra care required:**
- Baby formula: Must be sealed, unexpired
- Car seats: Must not be expired or recalled
- Cribs: Must meet safety standards
- Toys: Must be age-appropriate, safe

## 11. Reporting Violations

### 11.1 How to Report

If you see a prohibited item:
1. Click "Report" on the listing
2. Select the reason (prohibited item)
3. Provide details
4. Submit

### 11.2 What Happens Next

- Reports are reviewed within 24-48 hours
- Confirmed violations result in removal
- Serious violations reported to authorities
- Reporter identity kept confidential
