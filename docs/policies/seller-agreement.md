# Seller Agreement — Treido.eu

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose

This Seller Agreement (the "Agreement") establishes the terms and conditions under which you ("Seller," "You," or "Your") may sell goods through the Treido.eu platform ("Platform," "Treido," "We," or "Us").

### 1.2 Relationship to General Terms

This Agreement supplements the Treido.eu Terms of Service. In case of conflict, this Seller Agreement takes precedence for seller-specific matters.

### 1.3 Acceptance

By publishing a listing on Treido.eu, you confirm that you:
- Have read and accept this Agreement
- Meet all eligibility requirements
- Will comply with all applicable laws and regulations

---

## 2. Seller Eligibility

### 2.1 Minimum Requirements

To sell on Treido.eu, you must:
- Be at least 18 years old (or legal age in your jurisdiction)
- Have a valid account in good standing
- Have legal authority to sell the listed items
- Comply with all applicable laws in Bulgaria and the EU

### 2.2 Business Sellers

If you sell:
- More than 50 items per month, OR
- Items with total value exceeding €2,000 per month, OR
- As part of a registered business activity

You are considered a **Professional Seller** and must:
- Register a Business Account
- Provide business registration details (EIK, VAT if applicable)
- Comply with additional consumer protection obligations
- Display required business information on your profile

### 2.3 Prohibited Sellers

You may NOT sell on Treido.eu if you:
- Have been previously banned from the Platform
- Are subject to trade sanctions or restrictions
- Have a history of fraudulent activity
- Cannot legally enter into binding contracts

---

## 3. Listing Standards

### 3.1 Accuracy

Every listing must be:
- **Truthful:** Descriptions must accurately reflect the item
- **Complete:** Include all material information (defects, modifications, condition)
- **Current:** Information must be up-to-date

### 3.2 Photos

- Must show the **actual item** being sold (no stock photos)
- Must be clear and well-lit
- Must show any defects or damage
- Must not contain watermarks from other platforms
- Must not contain contact information (phone, email, URLs)

### 3.3 Pricing

- Prices must be stated in **EUR** (Euro)
- Prices must include all costs (or clearly state extras)
- Prices must be realistic (no placeholder prices like €0.01)
- "Negotiable" prices are allowed

### 3.4 Categories

- Items must be listed in the correct category
- Multi-category items should be in the most relevant category
- Treido may recategorize incorrectly listed items

### 3.5 Listing Limits

| Account Type | Active Listings | Photos per Listing | Listing Duration |
|--------------|-----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Free | 20 | 8 | 30 days |
| Premium | 100 | 15 | 60 days |
| Pro | 500 | 20 | 90 days |
| Business | 2000+ | 30 | Custom |

## 4. Prohibited Items

### 4.1 Absolutely Prohibited

The following items may **NEVER** be sold on Treido.eu:

**Illegal Items:**
- Drugs and drug paraphernalia
- Weapons, ammunition, explosives
- Stolen property
- Counterfeit goods and replicas
- Items violating intellectual property rights

**Dangerous Items:**
- Hazardous materials
- Recalled products
- Unregulated pharmaceuticals
- Certain chemicals and precursors

**Restricted Content:**
- Pornographic material
- Items promoting violence or hatred
- Human remains or body parts
- Live animals (most categories)

### 4.2 Regulated Items (May Require Verification)

Some items may be sold with restrictions:
- Vehicles (age restrictions, documentation)
- Alcohol (licensed sellers only)
- Tobacco products (prohibited to individuals)
- Collectible weapons (deactivated, with documentation)
- Real estate (subject to verification)

### 4.3 Full Prohibited Items List

See: [Prohibited Items and Services](/policies/prohibited-items)

## 5. Communication with Buyers

### 5.1 Response Expectations

Sellers should:
- Respond to inquiries within **24 hours** during business days
- Provide clear and honest answers
- Not pressure buyers into immediate decisions
- Maintain professional communication

### 5.2 Prohibited Communication

Sellers must NOT:
- Share personal contact information to avoid platform fees
- Redirect transactions to other platforms
- Send spam or bulk messages
- Use abusive or threatening language
- Request payment outside agreed methods

### 5.3 Information to Provide

When asked, sellers must provide:
- Additional photos (reasonable requests)
- Details about condition and history
- Availability and preferred meeting locations
- Any known defects or issues

## 6. Transactions (V1 — Off-Platform)

### 6.1 V1 Transaction Model

In the current version (V1), Treido.eu facilitates:
- Discovery (listing publication and search)
- Communication (messaging between parties)

Payment and delivery are arranged directly between Seller and Buyer **outside** the Platform.

### 6.2 Seller Responsibilities

As a Seller, you are solely responsible for:
- Describing items accurately
- Delivering goods as described
- Handling payment arrangements safely
- Resolving disputes with Buyers
- Complying with tax obligations

### 6.3 Recommended Practices

For safe transactions, we recommend:
- Meet in public, well-lit locations
- Accept payment upon item handover
- Avoid shipping high-value items to unknown buyers
- Keep records of transactions
- Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, don't proceed

### 6.4 Receipt/Documentation

For items over €100, we recommend:
- Written receipt signed by both parties
- Include: date, item description, price, condition
- Take photos at handover

## 7. Professional Seller Obligations

### 7.1 Consumer Protection Law Compliance

Professional Sellers must:
- Provide clear pre-contractual information
- Honor the **14-day withdrawal right** for remote sales
- Provide 2-year legal guarantee on goods
- Handle complaints promptly
- Issue invoices when requested

### 7.2 Required Information Display

Professional Sellers must display:
- Business name and legal form
- Registration number (EIK)
- Registered address
- Contact email and phone
- VAT number (if applicable)

### 7.3 Tax Obligations

Professional Sellers are responsible for:
- Registering for VAT if exceeding thresholds
- Declaring income and paying taxes
- Issuing proper invoices
- Maintaining accounting records

**Note:** Treido.eu does not provide tax advice. Consult a professional.
