# Returns & Refunds Policy

> Canonical Returns & Refunds policy for Treido. Served at `/[locale]/returns`.

---

## overview: Overview

Treido is a marketplace. Returns and refunds depend on the transaction, the item condition, and the evidence available.

## eligibility: Eligibility

Returns/refunds may be available when:

- the item is not received,
- the item is significantly not as described,
- the item is damaged in transit (with supporting evidence),
- other policy-defined conditions apply.

## timelines: Timelines

Users must report eligible issues within the timelines shown in Treido for the specific order.

## evidence: Evidence

Treido may request photos, packaging evidence, tracking information, and relevant messages to evaluate claims.

## outcomes: Outcomes

Outcomes may include:

- refund (full or partial),
- return workflow (when applicable),
- no refund when policy conditions are not met.
