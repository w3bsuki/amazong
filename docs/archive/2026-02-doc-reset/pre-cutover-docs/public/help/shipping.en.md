# Shipping (Help)

> Shipping expectations for buyers and sellers.

---

## overview: Overview

Treido supports marketplace transactions where the seller is responsible for packaging and shipping the item as described.

## sellers: Seller responsibilities

- Ship within the timeframe shown to the buyer (where applicable)
- Package items to reduce damage risk
- Provide tracking when available

## buyers: Buyer responsibilities

- Provide accurate delivery information
- Confirm receipt in a timely manner (where applicable)
- Report problems within defined windows
