## Feed Algorithm

### Content Mix (per screen)
1. **20%** - Promoted sellers (paid)
2. **30%** - Sellers you follow / interacted with
3. **25%** - Hot sellers in your area
4. **15%** - New sellers worth discovering
5. **10%** - AI-recommended based on browsing

### Ranking Signals
- Recent activity (new listings)
- Seller rating
- Response time
- Location proximity
- Category affinity (user's history)
- Engagement (views, saves, messages)
